The Smart Choice for Ambitious Students

Dual enrollment allows eligible high school students to take college-level courses and earn both high school and college credit simultaneously. Our "Introduction to Biotechnology Lab" course is an ideal way to leverage this opportunity, especially if you're interested in science, medicine, or research.

Get a Head Start on College

  • Earn transferable college credits: Reduce your course load in college and potentially graduate early.
  • Save on tuition costs: Dual enrollment courses are often free or significantly reduced compared to regular college tuition.
  • Experience college-level academics: Prepare for the rigor and expectations of university coursework in a supportive environment.

Boost Your College Applications

  • Demonstrate academic rigor: Show colleges you're ready for advanced studies.
  • Stand out from the crowd: Completing college-level science courses as a high school student is impressive.
  • Explore interests early: Confirm your passion for biotechnology before committing to a major in college.

Develop Advanced Skills

  • Hands-on lab proficiency: Gain practical skills rarely taught in high school.
  • Critical thinking & problem-solving: Apply scientific methodology to real-world challenges.
  • Networking opportunities: Connect with college faculty and like-minded peers.

Dual enrollment isn't just about credits; it's about building confidence, clarifying your academic path, and setting a strong foundation for future success.
