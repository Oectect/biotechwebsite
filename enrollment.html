Ready to Enroll? Your Path to Biotechnology Starts Here!

Follow these simple steps to secure your spot in the "Introduction to Biotechnology Lab" dual enrollment course.

Enrollment Steps for High School Students

We're thrilled you're interested in joining our cutting-edge Biotechnology Lab! The dual enrollment process ensures you receive both high school and college credit for this incredible experience.

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the basic requirements:

  • Current high school freshman, sophomore or junior.
  • Strong interest in science or STEM fields.
  • Recommended GPA 3.0 or higher, check with your high school counselor for specifics.

Step 2: Connect with SCC

The SCC representives are a primary resource:

  • Discuss your interest in the "Introduction to Biotechnology Lab" course.
  • Confirm the dual enrollment agreement between El Modena and SCC.
  • Obtain Special Amdit form for dual enrollment.

Step 3: Complete College Application

For dual enrollment, you need to:

  • Apply for SCC for the fall. Click here to apply
  • Once account has been created, you will be email with your account infomation.
  • Once you have your account infomation sign into self service

Step 4: Register for the Course

Once on self service:

  • Work with the college's dual enrollment representives to officially register for the "Introduction to Biotechnology Lab" course.
  • Confirm course schedule, location, and materials needed.

Important Note: Spaces are limited! It is encouraged to start this process as early as possible to secure a spot.
