About The Biotechnology Lab Course

A comprehensive introduction to modern biotechnology, designed specifically for ambitious high school students.

What You'll Experience

Our "Introduction to Biotechnology Lab" is a dual enrollment course offering high school students a unique opportunity to engage with college-level scientific practices. This course is designed to provide foundational knowledge and practical skills crucial for success in higher education and STEM careers.

Course Overview:

  • Immersive Lab Environment: Spend significant time in a state-of-the-art laboratory, replicating real-world research settings.
  • Expert Instruction: Learn from experienced instructors who are passionate about biotechnology and dedicated to student success.
  • Small Class Sizes: Benefit from personalized attention and ample opportunity for hands-on practice.
  • Project-Based Learning: Apply your knowledge to exciting projects, mirroring challenges faced by professional scientists.

Who Should Enroll?

This course is perfect for high school freshmen and sophomores (who will be juniors/seniors when they can take the course) who have a strong interest in:

  • Biology, Chemistry, or general Science
  • Medicine, healthcare, or pharmacology
  • Scientific research and discovery
  • Any STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) field
  • Gaining a competitive edge for college admissions

Course Goals:

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Demonstrate proficiency in fundamental lab techniques.
  • Understand the principles behind common biotechnological processes.
  • Accurately record and analyze scientific data.
  • Appreciate the ethical considerations in biotechnology.
  • Be better prepared for rigorous college-level science courses.
